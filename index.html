About the Game

Adventures With Anxiety is a unique interactive experience created for individuals dealing with anxiety disorder. Developed by someone who personally understands anxiety, this game uses humor and relatable scenarios to help players visualize how anxiety works.

Through its simple yet engaging stick figure art style and a hyperactive wolf companion, the game offers insights into the inner workings of anxiety while reducing the fear of fear itself.

Content Note

This game contains themes of anxiety, alcohol abuse, sexuality, and strong language. It's intended for a mature audience.
